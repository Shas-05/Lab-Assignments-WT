1. BURGER

Ingredients:

  • Burger Patty
  • Burger Buns
  • Veggies
    • Onion
    • Tomato
    • Cucumber
  • Spreads
    • Mayonnaise
    • Tandoori Sause
    • Mustard sause
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Prepare toppings with sliced tomato , onion and lettuce
  2. Heat the burger patty for about 3-5 mins
  3. Spread butter on the pan and toast the buns.
  4. Now put patty and all veggies on the bun.
  5. Top it up with cheese and spread as per your choice.
  6. Put the other side of bun on other and your burger is ready.


2. French Fries

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Water

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Slice potatoes into uniform sticks for even cooking.
  2. Soak for 30-60 mins to rem,ove starch and improve texture.
  3. First fry at lower temperature and heat at higher temperature for crispiness
  4. Use paper napkins to absorb excess oil after frying.
  5. Toss with salt and spices while still hot.


3. Coffee

Ingredients:

  • Ground coffee beans
  • Water
  • Optional Add-ins for Flavor & Texture
    • Milk or Cream
    • Sugar
    • Flavor Syrups
      • Vanilla
      • Hazelnut
      • Caramel
    • Cocoa Powder
    • Ice(for cold coffee)
    • Ice-Cream

Recipe Instructions:

  1. In a bowl mix coffeee powder , sugar and hot water
  2. Mix it thoroughly with a spoon for 5 mins.
  3. Boil the milk and pour it into the cup.
  4. Spoon the whipped milk over the coffee.
  5. Gently stir the mixture.
